Most Embarassed Time

Mar 29th, 2013

It was a day when I was in high school. I was having a Math class. I got bored so I went for a chat with my next-desk friend. We were chitty chatty quietly but eventually the teacher got us. Let’s call the teacher Yuan. She got us probably because we sat in the front of the classroom, so what we have done really disturbed her. She got so angry and wanted us to meet her after class. She used to be a nice and easy teacher. It was perhaps we talked too much and we have talked on every Math class before.

So after class we went to the teacher office to meet her. My friend and I stood there nervously, right in front of teacher Yuan while she was sitting at her desk trying to pretend she was mad about us. I knew that because I knew her. The tension was released a bit, no one got pulished. My friend stood aside me is a funny boy, that’s why we often talked on class. He found that Yuan was not that angry so he started to say something funny. I don’t remember what he said, but I do remember very clearly what was happened next. Because I was so released so I laughed so hard that a black/yellow bogus was shot out of my nose right to Yuan’s jeans.

One second of silent at that office, then my friend explode to laugh. I felt so embarrassed that I was trying to wipe that bogus off Yuan’s jeans. However that bogus was not completely dry. It was a little sticky, like a half ripe rice with a hard core. So all I was doing was not wiping it off, but spreading it all over Yuan’s knee.

My friend almost died that he leaned himself on the side of the desk. I wanted to laugh too, but I knew that it was not appropriate because I am the one that spread that bogus. I slowly raised my head, facing Yuan’s face which is mixed with all kinds of emotions: fury, but holding laugh. Then I stopped wiping.

As I remembered, that was the only right thing I did in that office that day.

字符串处理【1】- 回文

Mar 22nd, 2013

回文(Palindrome)是一个平时开发不怎么会遇到，但面试时经常会被问到的一个问题。所谓回文，意思是一串字符或数字正读和反着读都是相同的。比如abcba, 11111。回文因其解法的多样性常被用来考察应聘者的能力，针对回文也可以问出各种各样的问题。

初级：判断是否是回文

给一个字符串或数字，判断其是否是回文。数字处理要稍微比字符串麻烦些，不过和高级的找回文想必就简单了许多。

字符串处理【0】

Mar 6th, 2013

字符串处理问题里有很多种最长问题：最长相同子字符串，最长连续序列，最长回文，尽管都称为最长，但其解法确实不尽相同。不过，有一种强大的数据结构可以用来解决所有这些字符串问题，这种数据结构叫做suffix tree.

因为很强大，所以比一般的binary tree什么的复杂了许多。我自己就花了很长时间来学习。但各个最长问题都有其自己的不用suffix tree的解法。所以，在接下来几篇post里，会依次introduce各问题的用数据结构的解法，和不用数据结构的解法。希望这个系列能彻底让我搞定字符串处理的问题。
